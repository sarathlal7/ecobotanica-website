About Ecobotanica 🌿

Our Initiative

Ecobotanica Biodegradable Products LLP was founded with a clear mission — to support a clean and plastic-free world. We believe businesses should take responsibility in reducing environmental impact.

Our Purpose

Plastic pollution is one of the biggest challenges today. Ecobotanica is our initiative to provide eco-friendly alternatives that help reduce plastic usage and promote sustainable living.

Our Product

We currently offer areca leaf plates, made from naturally fallen leaves. These are 100% biodegradable, chemical-free, and safe for the environment.

Our Vision

We aim to expand into more biodegradable products and contribute to a greener future, both locally and globally.
